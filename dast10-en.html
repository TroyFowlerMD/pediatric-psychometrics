DAST-10

Drug Abuse Screening Test — screens for problematic drug use in adolescents. Does not include alcohol.

10 items Clinical cutoff: ≥3 Scale: Yes/No

Instructions

The following questions refer to drug use in the LAST 12 MONTHS. Does not include alcohol. 'Drug abuse' includes: overuse of prescription or OTC medications, and any non-medical use of drugs (cannabis, cocaine, stimulants, hallucinogens, narcotics, tranquilizers, etc.).
